Description

OxGEn Molecular Solution Processed Meat DNA Purification Kit is simple and rapid system for high quality DNA preparation using a rapid spin column protocol. Kit contains sufficient materials for 50 purification. High molecular weight genomic DNA purified with the kit is suitable for direct use in various molecular biology applications: PCR, qPCR, southern blot analysis, sequencing and more. The protocol can be completed including incubation time in approximately 60 minutes.
